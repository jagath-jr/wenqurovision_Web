Our Services

At Max Miller General Contracting LLC, we have been delivering excellence in the construction industry since 2004. With over two decades of experience, we provide end-to-end solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects across the region. Our expertise spans construction, demolition, building maintenance, MEP services, civil works, interiors, steel fabrication, and aluminum & glass works, ensuring that every project is handled with precision, safety, and quality.

From concept to completion, our dedicated team combines innovative solutions, sustainable practices, and timely execution to meet the unique needs of every client, making us a trusted partner in the construction industry.

Aluminium and Glass Works

Enhance the look and functionality of your building with our aluminum and glass solutions. We design and install façades, windows, and partitions that combine modern aesthetics with durability and energy efficiency.

Construction & Demolition

Our construction and demolition services ensure safe, efficient, and high-quality results for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. From site preparation to structural demolition, we follow strict safety protocols and use advanced techniques to deliver projects on time and with minimal disruption.

Building Maintenance

We provide comprehensive building maintenance solutions to keep your property in top condition. Our services include preventive inspections, repairs, and facility upkeep to enhance safety, extend the building’s life, and maintain its value.

Swimming pool Maintenance

Our professional swimming pool maintenance services keep your pool sparkling clean, hygienic, and safe. From water treatment and cleaning to equipment checks, we ensure your pool remains in perfect condition year-round.

Civil Works

We handle all types of civil works, including foundation construction, earthworks, and infrastructure development. Our team combines technical expertise with a commitment to quality, ensuring every project meets the highest industry standards.

Interior & fit-Out Works

Transform your spaces with our custom interior and fit-out services. We design and execute tailored solutions for offices, commercial spaces, and residential properties, blending functionality, aesthetics, and modern finishes.

MEP Works

Our Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) services are designed to ensure seamless building operations. From installations to maintenance, we provide energy-efficient and reliable solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Steel Fabrication

We deliver precision-engineered steel fabrication services for structural, industrial, and architectural applications. Our durable and high-quality steel solutions are tailored to meet the exact needs of every project.
